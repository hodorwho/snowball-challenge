Our mission

Through our app, we want to make sure the people working on our clothes get the payment they're earned:

It has been uncovered that a lot of people working in this industry works under hazardous conditions and gets paid almost nothing for it. This app is a way to show our gratitude towards the one who made our clothes through being able to give them money directly. A couple of dollars isn't a lot to you, but it can be life changing for her.

So, are you ready to make a big difference?
